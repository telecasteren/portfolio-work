I grew up in Fredrikstad, a small city on the east shoreside of Norway. Whilst growing up I never really saw myself going anywhere. Couldn't even see past tomorrows school bell ringing.. When turning 16 I started working in the city's only proper coffee shop at the time, and by the time I was 18 I was there fulltime. I worked hard, participated in several coffee courses, and was taught by the great Norwegian and World champion Tim Wendelboe.

During my journey, there is a line that keep repeating itself, learning by doing.
