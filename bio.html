my walk of life.

I grew up in Fredrikstad, a small city on the southeast shoreside of Norway. Whilst growing up I never really saw myself going anywhere. Couldn't even see past tomorrows school bell ringing.. When turning 16 I started working in the city's only proper coffee shop at the time, and by the time I was 18 I was there fulltime. I worked hard, participated in several coffee courses, and was taught by Norwegian- and World Barista and coffee brewing champions.

But I never began competing professionally myself.. Why?

During my journey, there is a line that keep repeating itself, learning by doing. And this extends to getting to know myself. So over the years I started to get to know the me, and what I actually wanted in my life, through life and of life.
This eventually brought me to programming. Creative and hands on projects that would put me in a creative position, be a part of product, and actually be able to use my already well trained business and customer service skills.

What do I stand for and what can I offer?
